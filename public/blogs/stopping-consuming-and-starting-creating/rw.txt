# Intangiriro

Nahize njiha ijambo kuri njye mu ntangiriro y'umwaka gukora ibigize ibiganiro, ariko ubu ni Werurwe 15 kandi ntabwo nakoze byinshi.

Iyi blog izaba igisohoka cyanjye ku bice byakize, nta byakize n'ibintu bisanzwe bitagize/byakize nkakagira nka nkwigire uko nagomba kurwanya.

Mu myezi yishize nari gukoresha inzira n'intera z'ibigize ibiganiro zitandukanye.

- Ibigize akazi
- Ibigize cy'ubwanzi bwujuje
- Ibigize byuzuye

Ubu niyumva ko ndifite ibipimo neza ku bice byakize n'ibitakize kuva ku mbare n'amadidiko. Ibice byakize cyane nkuri mwacu ni iyo ntimunyuja amagambo, aho nkamakuru cyangwa ikwigire ry'ibinyamakuru.

Umwanzi, naragize 3 cyangwa 4 reel nkivuga ividini givuga ibigize byuzuye, byatagiranye neza.

Ariko ubwana naragize rim rim imwe ku ntumbero yanjye (amagambo) ku buyobozi bw'amafaranga kandi yagarukanye neza. Ariko kube ari ukuri, nkigira igisohoka cy'Igiswe kigenza cyiza cyitamanisha.

Ibigize nkibikora bifite gushakira kwiyumvisha bikunze gushakira.

# Nkurikirana ba miryango yanjye

Natangiye kwiyigira mu matanira y'ababyeyi bisanzwe mu Londoni, kuva icyo gihe nabakunye abamusozi bamwe bari gukora ibigize ibiganiro. Mu munyaja wabo, umwe yabikuze 10x abantu bakunze kurikira kuruta uko nabikoze mu myezi itatu.

Nzi ko ntabwo ari neza kwikinagira munyamunyaji, ariko niyumva ko gukoramira abantu bamwe bakora ibigize bisa nubwo kugira igitekerezo kandi ntikwigire ni nzira nziza.

Nazihanzi indugu imwe ifite ubwiyunge bworere neza, ifite inzira y'ubwanzi bwacu buri gitondo kandi yakunze abaguzi 50k mu myezi yishize - iyo nari gutangira yari ifite abantu 3k.

Ari ubwigunge bwigisha ko bikunze gukura, nagomba guhitamo gute.

Gukoramira abo nari kumenyeza ibigize byakize ku bice nabakora bakize nkikwigire nkagomba gukora.

Hano hari ibigize nkibike:

- Ubwigunge buri gitondo ifite akamaro kanini, abantu nazihanzi bari bagukura abantu bari gukora ibigize buri gitondo mu nzira yabo.

- Imvugo z'amagambo, abamusozi nazihanzi barwanya amagambo neza. Bari kumeza, nta bo bikwigire bitagize - usually bari kumeza irihe gishakira kandi kumvugo amagambo.

- Igishakira c'iwandiko ryo hejuru, iyo ibigize byanjye ntabwo bifite igishakira cyijwi, abantu ntibikubita. Ibigize nta ibitabo - abantu bikubita mu mahoro yacyo.

- Ikibazo cy'ubwigunge, abantu ntibiciriza ubwigunge iyo gikomeza ibigize bigize. Ubu niyigira ibigize k'ubushakashatsi kwa n'ubwanzi nkacyo itangira ariko ntageneza. Nagurangije igice gito 1-2 min ibigize, ubu irebera ko ibigize 30 cimatike bikunze neza.

- Igihe cy'ubwigunge, ibigize bikunze kwigira saa nke cyangwa nishyira mu mabantu kuva ku kunera, gutorosha n'gukoragaza. Nta nzi iyo ari ukuri ko nari mbageza, ibi bikunze kugira akamaro, ariko irebera ko nagomba kubyongeza mu gitondo cy'ubwanzi. Nazihanzi abamusozi bari na ba mugumire n'inyungu zabo. Nta nzi iyo ntagukinamo, ariko ubu irebera ko nagomba gukoragaza buri gitondo - urulamutse nkicira amafaranga kugira ngo abantu barugire.

- Guca gukora ibigize, nari kumenyeza TikTok/Instagram ibigize buri gitondo. Nagomba kuguca iyo nkigira gukundeza. Hari ubwiyunge bwanjye nagomba kubyemeza - guca gukora ibigize kandi gutangira gukundeza.

# Inzira nshya

Ubu nari gusubiza kandi niyumva nagomba gushyira mu mahoro ubwiyunge bwanjye bw'ibigize. Nagomba gucyatira igihe mu gitondo nkakora buri gitondo. Nagomba gukora amagambo, kumeza ibintu, kwigira nubwigunge buri gitondo n'gutura cwane igihe mu kumenyeza ibigize byabandi.

Ibigize nkibigira nzima ni ubwigunge, nkayo yindi mpunzi, nagomba kwigishwa nkwacu nkwacu.

Iyo niwigire code nkagira umwigire w'ubwanzi w'akucyara igihe 4 saa kwa gitondo bikoze ibigize, nagomba gukora ibigize nk'iki. Nagomba gukoragaza igihe 2 saa buri gitondo kunyambaza ibigize mbere yo gukora ibindi.

Ubu nari gutenga ofisi mu myezi itatu nkigihembwe kuruta gukunzira mu nzu. Niyumva ko iki kizaba ikigeza neza. Ubu nari kumusanga, kurya, gukwegeza kugera Londoni, kugera mu nzu kandi kunyambaza ibigize ritangira. Ubwana nkakora akazi kamwe ku gitondo kandi ntakugomba kuranura akazi gukoragaza reel cyangwa gukoragaza reel ikumvisha irashe.

Gukoramira mu matwi n'ubwigunge, cyangwa nagomba kwandika ibigize mu gari yo hasi, kuryizikiza naho gikijya ongera mu nzu kandi gukoragaza kimwe. Ubwana nkakora irinyo.

Iyo nigiciriza ibigize nshya niyumva ko nagomba gutemera akakorokoro karemerereza. Kuva ubu ijoro ryanjye rya buri gitondo rizaba:

- Kunyambaza ibigize ritangira buri gitondo gukugira ngo rikoze kandi gucyatira ubwigunge bwihuse cyane. Ubwana nkakora umukwigire w'ubwanzi nko kurasa amenye
- Nagomba gukora amagambo, kuba impunzi mu mamagambo kandi gukora ibigize bishakira kandi bikwigire.

Cyangwa nari gutaza nzira nziza, ariko niyumva ko gushyira mu mahoro ni ibigize byanjye ubu. Ubu nari gucoragaza mu myezi michache mu inzira nshya kandi kumenyeza ko iba ite. Ariko niyumva ko ari nzira nziza ko nari kubaka ibigize kandi ntakuregera mu gihe kigera.

Nagomba gukomeeza kandi gupiga nzira.
