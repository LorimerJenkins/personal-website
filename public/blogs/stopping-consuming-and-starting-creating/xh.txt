# Isingeniso

Ndathembisa kumdlali wam ekuqaleni kweminyaka ukuqala ukwenza izithuba, ngoku ilitye leshumi elisihlanu kunye nesithupha kunye nendlela engu-2026 kwaye andizange ndaqale uphakamelo olukhulu.

Ibhloghi olu luza kusebenza njengeenkcazo zam ezinesichitshelo sezinto ezisebenzile, ezingasebenzi kunye nezinto ezingasebenzi/ezisebenzile ukuze ndikwazi ukubona ukuba ini endinokuyilungisa.

Kwiinyanga ezimbalwa zokuthile bendiyithandela okukhethekileyo kwiindlela zezithuba/izitayile.

- Umsebenzi wesithuba
- Isikhutshelo sangempela
- Amaqondo anamhlanje

Ndinolwazi oluhle kwakhona ukuba yintoni eyasebenza kwaye yintoni engasebenzi kwiikhawunti zokubuka kunye nokuthanda. Izinto ezibonakalisayo ukuba zisebenza kum yi-ixesha apho ndixoxa iintsomi, apho ndim simbi kuphela kwisithuba.

Umzekelo, ndaqala iireeli ezintathu okanye ezine ndisithi endikubonile kwichimera elinikezelo lolwazi, zahluleka.

Kodwa kambe ndaqala ireeli enye ngokuqokelela ixesha lam elinye (intsomi) ngokuqokelela izimali kunye nolwazi kwaye ibonakala ifumanisa iikhauni eliphezulu. Kodwa, mna ndikubekele ne-American accent oyinzinte ndingathi ileo ayinike injongo.

Isithuba esndiqokelela endokuqala esindisebenzisane nokukunika injongo yokuxoxwa nokuthandwa kunokusebenza ngakumbi.

# Ukujonga iindlela zam

Ndibesiqalela inxalenye yemidlali engakumbi ngokwenziwa kwezithuba kwilondon, ukusukela pho ndiye ndihlanganiswe nezilwa ezininzi eziqalela izikhutshelo. Ndiku ndive nabo, omnye wazo ayafumene iindidi ezitshumi ngaphezulu kunene kunyanga enye endisenza izithuba zemihlela kuphela, ngaphandle kweenyanga ezintathu.

Ndiyaqonda ukuba ayikho into entle ukuzijolulisa nabanye, kodwa ndinomava ojambulula, ukujonga abantu abathemba into efanayo noyintoni kwam kunokufunela imiyalelo, ayeso nokuzilwela omnye endinokuqala.

Ndiyaqonda nokuqokelela mnye umhlobo onolwimi oluncedisayo, unolungu olugqwesileyo ubudlwane benye imihla kwaye uqokelele iikhawunti ezingaphezu kwe-50k ikakhulu emalanga engakuqala-ene-3k.

Kuyaphela kakhulu ukuba uqokelele iikhawunti, kwaye ndidinga kuphela ukufumanela nokuba wenzela.

Ukujonga kuzo ndiyaziqalela nokufunela yokuba zenzani ezenza umtyandazo kufumana iikhawunti endikufunela.

Zaneli iingomso:

- Ubudlelwane bemihlela yonke ibonakala yikona isixhobo esikhulu, abantu abaqonda ndiyabafumene abayaphucula izikhutshelo ngemihla yonke kumvumelwano.

- Intsomi, abaqokelela izikhutshelo abaqonda ukufumana iikhawunti bayenxulumene nemikhosi. Baqokelela, abangabo abantu abangaxutywe - badinga ukuqokelela amaqabane kunye nokutsho imikhosi.

- Ifoto yokuqala iqala, ukuba iireeli zam ayinankqo entle, abantu ayinizonggq izonggqo. Iireeli ayizizo ezincwadi, abantu ayijolulisa ngefoto yayo.

- Inkxaso-mali yexesha, abantu ayinikizeleli ixesha ngaxesha lokugqwalela iireeli zambi ividiyo eyolemayini. Njengoko ndiqalela nokufunda izekelisiso zexesha ngokuqala ngaloo, iindlela ezinzima zereeli azicebile kuze ndikwazi ukufumanela okusebenzayo kunokusebenzayo. Ndiqalela nge-1-2 imizuzu yereeli, ngoku ibonakala i-30s iireeli ziqokelela ncam kakhulu.

- Ixesha lokuziqalela, iireeli zam siza kuthathela iiyure ezimbalwa ukuqala nokulungelelanisa nokuthumela. Andiyazi ukuba ayikho nto enca andikulungisele, enokuba inzima, kodwa ibonakala ndidinga yokuphumla ngakumbi kumsuku kunye nokuziqalela - kunokuba ndimbe nokuba ndiya ndikhokelela. Ndiyamazi abaqokelela izikhutshelo abanothele iihlobo kunye neendlu eziza kumxhashi. Andiyithalathaleli ukuthemba ngen endlakufundo kutamanini, kodwa ngoku ibonakala ndidinga ukuphumla ncam kumsuku kunye nokuziqalela - kunokuba ndimbe nokuba ndiya ndikhokelela.

- Uyeke ukulucula iireeli, ndilusela ngantetho i-TikTok/Instagram iireeli nemihlela yonke. Ndidinga ukuyeka ukuba ndifune ukuba ngumqokeleli.

# Isiqingci esitsha

Ndiqalela nje kwaye ndiyumva ndidinga ukwenza uguqulo olunzima ngendlela andisebenzisele isithuba. Ndidinga yokuphumla ngaxesha kumsuku kunye nokuziqalela yonke imihla. Ndidinga ukwenza imikhosi, uqokelela imiqokelelo, ukunqanda ngokukhawula kunye nokuxoka ixesha kum yonke imihla ukujonge isithuba sebanthumanazela.

Into endiyifumana inzima kakhulu yibudlelwane, njengoko nifunda nto, ndidinga ukuziqalela, ukuziqalela, ukuziqalela.

Nkanger ndizithe ukulwa ngekhadi, ndiqokelela indlela yondlela yemihlela eninzi yezijizela amalanga neminyaka engu-4 yexesha kumvumelwano, ndidinga ukwenza into engathi iyayo esithubeni. Ndidinga yokuphumla kumlungu amalanga omava emini kunye nokuziqalela ekuseni kusoze ndenze nto enye ukuqinisekisa eyiphiwe.

Ngoku ndiya ndiqalela ukuthenga i-ofisi emva kweminyaka esihlanu kumthwalo endisebenzele kumlungu. Ndinomava oku kuza kube iinguqulo ezikholiswa.

Ndiya ndikuphumla, ndikudla, ndisandi elondonini, ndiye ndiye ofisini ndiqalela isithuba kuqala. Emva koko ndenze umsebenzi wam onganeno ngaxesha kunye nenani kunye nokuqedwa umsebenzi ngokukhawula ukulungelelanisa nereeli okanye ndereli eyolemayini.

Ngaxesha ekuboneni amanye nto ndiphi apho ndiqokelela nto entle kumntla womvume.

Emva koko indawo entle yentlobo yam eya kube:


- Ukwenza isithuba yokuqala emini ukuze kuqinisekiswe eyiphiwe kwaye ichaza ixesha eliqhubekileyo lokuziqalela. Emva koko eyiphiwe kwaye noba indawo emandi engathi ukunqanda amazinyo
- Ndidinga ukwenza imikhosi, ba mqokeleli wemikhosi kwaye ndenze isithuba elikholiswa ukunqanda kunye nolwimi.

Ndiyakwazi ukuba ndiphumle endleleni engafanelekiyo apha, kodwa ndiyumva ukwenza uguqulo nkwazimka ngoku. Ndiya ndibhala iinyanga ezimbalwa endleleni entsha kunye nokujonga nokuba yeyakho. Kodwa ndiyumva ezilungile ukuba ndisathandela okuziqalela kunye nokayilo emveni kwesikamelo.

Ndidinga kuphela kuqhubeka kunye nokayilo.
